# Welcome to Verso

Verso is a mesh of [[Projects/Obsidian]] and [[Projects/Logseq]] — a local-first
notebook built on plain Markdown.

Start here, then wander into [[Ideas]] or peek at what the team is building in
[[Projects/Verso]]. People you'll meet: [[People/Ada]] and [[People/Linus]].

## What works today

- [[wikilinks]] with autocomplete (type `[[`)
- Backlinks at the bottom of every page
- A graph of the whole vault
- A database view over note frontmatter
