# PDF Annotation

Click the link below to open the PDF annotator. It opens the viewer in one pane
with a highlights panel on the side.

[sample.pdf](assets/sample.pdf)

## How it works
- Select text in the PDF, then pick a highlight color from the little toolbar.
- The highlight appears in the **Highlights** panel on the right.
- Click a highlight in the panel to **jump back to that spot** in the PDF.
- Hit **⧉ ref** on a highlight to copy a link like `[p.1](hl:…)` — paste it into
  any note, and clicking it reopens the PDF at that highlight.

You can also **drag a PDF** straight into any note to file it under `assets/`.
